Problem

Payments Are Still Broken in the Real World

Scanning QR codes, entering UPI IDs, and waiting for confirmations slows everything down. In fast-moving environments like autos and street commerce, payments should be instant — not a process.

01

QR codes go missing

Printed stands tear, fade, or never show up where the payment moment happens.

02

Manual UPI entry is slow

Typing IDs in an auto or on a crowded roadside adds friction to every ride and sale.

03

People hesitate mid-payment

Customers double-check names, merchants repeat details, and queues begin to form.

04

Local commerce needs speed

Autos, small shops, and vendors need a payment flow optimized for quick, repeat use.

Solution

Meet SPAROO — Payments That Move With You

SPAROO eliminates friction from everyday payments. No scanning. No manual entry. Just enter, detect, and pay instantly.

How It Works

Designed for a Single, Seamless Payment Motion

From entering a vehicle to completing payment — everything happens automatically in seconds.

1

Device broadcasts ID

Each driver or merchant carries a SPAROO device that shares a secure BLE identity.

2

App detects nearby merchant

The customer app listens for nearby verified SPAROO devices and matches the closest one.

3

User taps pay

The app shows merchant details, the customer confirms, and the payment can begin instantly.

4

Payment via Razorpay

Settlement runs on trusted payment rails while SPAROO removes the discovery friction.

5

Merchant receives money

The partner gets paid immediately and sees the transaction in their dashboard.
